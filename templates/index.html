"Did you know that according to the 2022 Kenya Demographic Health Survey, 16% of women in Kenya have experienced physical violence since the age of 15, and 13% have experienced sexual violence at some point in their lives?"

Despite this worrying numbers, the perpetrators are rarely brought to justice.For instance, a 2022 study in Mombasa County found that of all reported cases of sexual assault, only 57.7% proceeded to court. Of the cases that actually reached a judgment, a worrying 23% were either withdrawn or ended in acquittal. This high rate of case attrition is often due to a lack of forensic evidence, poor witness testimonies, and survivors withdrawing their cases, underscoring the systemic barriers to justice.
